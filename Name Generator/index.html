Random Brand Name Generator

It's frustrating when you are ready to practice your design skills, but can't think a fictional brand name to play with. Click the button below to automatically generate a fake word for your next creative session

Your new name can be used for Logo Designs or perhaps a full UI Design! You could even apply your newly created name to Packaging and Product Design to really show off your skills.

This app was built by Amy Sherburn in 2021. Every name is randomly generated, and any similarities to existing copywrited names is unintentional. These words should not be used for commercial or official purposes and are purely for personal use.
